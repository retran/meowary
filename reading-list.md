# Reading List

Books, articles, and resources to read — with resonance tracking.

## How to Use the Notes Column

The **Notes** column is not optional filler. Use it to capture resonance — your reaction to the material — so future-you can prioritize and connect ideas.

**What to record:**
- **Why it matters now** — what problem or question makes this relevant today.
- **Key insight** — after reading, the single most useful idea in one sentence.
- **Connects to** — link to resource articles, projects, or other reading list items it relates to.
- **Reaction** — agree, disagree, surprised, already knew, want to test.
