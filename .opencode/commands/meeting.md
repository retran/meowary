Record meeting notes. This is an interactive process.

Arguments: `/meeting [project-slug]`

1. Load `writing` skill and `writing/meeting` sub-skill.
2. Follow the meeting workflow from the skill:
   - Gather details: title, project/area, attendees, duration.
   - Take notes: discussion, decisions, action items.
   - Write to `journal/meetings/YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>.md` from template.
   - Cross-link to daily note and project READMEs.
   - KB scan: update resource articles if durable facts were learned.
   - Commit.

$ARGUMENTS
